This journal records CRITICAL UX/accessibility learnings.

## 2024-05-22 - Mobile Menu Accessibility
**Learning:** Mobile menu toggle buttons are often overlooked for accessibility. Without `aria-label` and state indicators, they are unusable for screen reader users.
**Action:** Always verify mobile navigation accessibility, specifically `aria-label`, `aria-expanded`, and `aria-controls` on toggle buttons.
